This game reads the plate's left-vs-right load split. Standing on both feet ≈ 50/50; lift a leg and your weight pins to the standing side. The clock runs while it stays there and stops the instant your other foot touches down. Calibrate the plate first (in the main game's Advanced → Calibration) so the loads read true.
